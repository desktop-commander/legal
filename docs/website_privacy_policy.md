# Website Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 18, 2025**

## Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Desktop Commander ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website at desktopcommander.app and related subdomains.

For information about data collection in the Desktop Commander software itself, please see our [Software Privacy Policy](./mcp-privacy-policy.md).

## What We Collect

### Website Analytics

We use [PostHog](https://posthog.com) (hosted in the EU) to understand how visitors use our website. This includes:

- Pages visited and time spent
- Clicks and interactions
- Referrer information (how you found us)
- Device type, browser, and operating system
- Approximate location (country/region level)

We respect the "Do Not Track" browser setting. If enabled, we will not track your activity.

### Newsletter Subscription

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. We use [HubSpot](https://hubspot.com) to manage our email communications. We will only send you emails about Desktop Commander updates, new features, and tips. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

### Blog

Our blog at blog.desktopcommander.app is hosted on WordPress.com, which has its own [privacy policy](https://automattic.com/privacy/).

## Cookies

We use cookies for:

- **Analytics:** To understand website usage (PostHog)
- **Functionality:** To remember your preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect some website functionality.

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Improve our website and understand what content is useful
- Send you updates about Desktop Commander (if subscribed)
- Respond to your inquiries
- Detect and prevent technical issues

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

- **PostHog:** Analytics processing (EU servers)
- **HubSpot:** Email delivery and management
- **WordPress.com:** Blog hosting

These services process data according to their own privacy policies and our data processing agreements.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Opt out of analytics tracking (via browser DNT setting)
- Unsubscribe from marketing emails
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

## Data Retention

We retain analytics data for up to 2 years. Email subscription data is retained until you unsubscribe, after which it is deleted within 30 days.

## Children

Our website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify subscribers of significant changes via email.

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us at:
