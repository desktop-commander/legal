# Cookie Policy - Desktop Commander

**Last updated:** January 22, 2026

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide a better user experience.

## How we use cookies

Desktop Commander uses cookies to provide essential functionality and to understand how visitors use our platform. This helps us improve your experience and make our product better.

We use two types of cookies:

### Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for Desktop Commander to function properly. They enable core features like authentication, security, and session management. You cannot disable these cookies as they are required for the platform to work.

**Examples include:**
- Authentication tokens that keep you logged in
- Security cookies that protect against malicious activity
- Session cookies that remember your actions during your visit

### Statistics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with Desktop Commander by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us identify areas for improvement and understand which features are most valuable to our users.

**Examples include:**
- Analytics cookies that track page views and user interactions
- Performance monitoring cookies that help us identify technical issues

We only set statistics cookies after you give us your consent.

## Managing your cookie preferences

You have full control over which cookies you accept:

### Change your preferences anytime

You can modify your cookie settings at any time by clicking the **"Cookie Settings"** link in our website footer. This will reopen the cookie banner where you can update your preferences.

### Browser settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:
- View what cookies are stored
- Delete existing cookies
- Block some or all cookies
- Delete cookies when you close your browser

Please note that blocking necessary cookies will prevent Desktop Commander from functioning properly.

Here are links to cookie management instructions for common browsers:
- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)
